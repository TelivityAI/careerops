4 · AI (nothing to set up — keys optional)

Free AI — no key needed. CareerOps includes a free AI model, 30 uses per day per person: fit scoring, resume tailoring, cover letters and chat all work out of the box. Your text is sent to the model provider to generate the answer; it is not used to train any AI model.

For higher-quality writing and no daily limit, add your own key — Anthropic (Claude) or Kimi K3, whichever you prefer. Your keys stay private to your login.

Claude: console.anthropic.com → API Keys. Kimi: platform.kimi.ai. Billed to you, cents per use. If both are saved, Claude is used.

Adds a “Humanize wording” button so AI-written text reads naturally. Only if you have an ai-text-humanizer.com account.

5 · How you'll actually use it

Finding jobs: hit 🔍 Run job search on your board — it scans the live career boards of 75+ companies for senior roles matching what you entered above, and drops them into “Sourced.” Drag cards across the board as you progress.

Resumes & cover letters — click any role card. A panel opens with that job's description already loaded. From there:
• Get match report — free score + missing keywords, saved onto the card (your built-in Jobscan).
• ✨ Tailor resume / ✉️ Cover letter — writes a version for that exact job (needs your Anthropic key) — then ⬇︎ download as Word right there.
• Everything you generate is saved with that role — cards with documents show 📄.
• Have a real Jobscan report? Paste it into the same panel — it's kept with the role.

Applying: the panel's Apply — Open JD ↗ button opens the real posting → apply on the company's site and attach the resume you just downloaded. The board tracks your search; you submit each application yourself.

Your data: it's private and yours alone — we can't access it. You can export everything anytime for your records from Settings → Your data — do that before you ever stop using the tool.
